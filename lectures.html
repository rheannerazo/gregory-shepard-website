What It Is

The map before the territory.

Orientation, not inspiration.

Most founders enter the startup world without a map. They build before they validate, scale before they standardize, raise before they're ready. They fail for the same predictable reasons that have been documented for over 20 years. The Overview Lecture gives you the map: all seven phases of the Startup Lifecycle, where they begin, what each one requires, and what kills founders at each stage.

  • Not a webinar with slides you'll forget by Thursday
  • Not motivational content dressed up as strategy
  • Not a sales pitch disguised as education
  • Built on real research, not theory

What's Covered

Seven phases. One direction. No shortcuts.

Each phase has a specific job. Skip one and the next one breaks. The lecture covers all seven: what they are, what they require, and what failure looks like at each stage.

Vision: Where are you going, and who eventually buys you?
Product: Building proof, not just a product
Go-to-Market: Predictable, repeatable traction
Standardize: Making the business run without you
Optimize: Efficient, scalable unit economics
Growth: Scaling without breaking the system
Exit: Becoming irresistible to your ideal acquirer
