What it is

The map before the territory.

Most founders enter the startup world without a map. They build before they validate. They scale before they standardize. They raise before they're ready — and they fail for the same predictable reasons documented for over 20 years.

In this masterclass, Gregory Shepard walks you through all seven phases of the Startup Lifecycle: where each one begins, what it requires, what kills founders at each stage, and what it takes to reach the other side. This is not inspiration. It's orientation — the single most important hour of clarity you can have before, or during, your startup journey.

What you'll walk away with

  • A complete view of all seven lifecycle phases — and exactly where you are right now
  • The failure patterns that kill startups at each stage, so you can see them coming
  • The frameworks behind 12 real exits and $1B+ in outcomes
  • A clear next move for your phase — plus live Q&A with Gregory

What this is not

  • A webinar with slides you'll forget by Thursday
  • Motivational content dressed up as strategy
  • A sales pitch disguised as education
  • Theory without real exits and real research behind it
