How It Works

Four conversations before a seat.

Fit matters more than speed.

Board fit is too consequential for a rushed process. Every potential engagement runs through the same four-step assessment before Gregory accepts a formal role.

  • Discovery call — a 45-minute introduction with the founder, CEO, lead investor, or recruiter
  • Due diligence — cap table, the last four board decks, financials, and any active M&A activity, references both directions
  • Founder fit — a two-hour working session on a real problem, testing operating chemistry both directions
  • Formal seat — engagement letter, equity terms, board approval, and fiduciary onboarding, typically 30–45 days from first call
