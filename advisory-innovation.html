How It Works

Four phases from inquiry to delivery.

Making sure the build is buildable first.

Innovation engagements take more architectural work up front than typical advisory — the goal of the opening phases is confirming the build is real before the engagement commits.

  • Discovery — a conversation with the executive sponsor on what the parent is trying to build, what's failed, and what's at stake
  • Architectural read — a review of the current innovation portfolio, org chart, and capital structure, returning a one-page structural diagnosis
  • Engagement design — a recommendation of Strategy, Spinout, or CVC model, with scope, timeline, and deliverables
  • Build begins — engagement starts, with cadence varying by type: weekly steerco for spinouts, monthly for portfolio strategy
