Guided by The Startup Lifecycle

Seven phases. Seven contexts.

Vision — validate the idea, define the problem worth solving
Product — build the MVP, test assumptions in market
Go-to-Market — find product–market fit, acquire the first real customers
Standardization — build repeatable processes, hire intentionally
Optimization — scale systems, remove the friction the early days hid
Growth — expand markets, think about what's next deliberately
Exit — M&A, IPO, or the next venture, positioned cleanly
