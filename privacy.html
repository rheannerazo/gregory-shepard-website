Privacy Policy.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This website, gregoryshepard.com ("the Site"), is the personal website of Gregory Shepard. Your privacy matters, and this page explains plainly what the Site does and does not do with information about you.

What this Site collects

The Site is a static informational website. It does not require accounts, does not set its own tracking cookies, and does not itself store personal information you type.

  • Hosting logs. The Site is served by a web hosting provider, which may automatically log standard technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages requested. That data is handled under the host's own privacy policy.
  • Fonts. Pages load typefaces from Google Fonts, which means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when a page loads. That request is subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Email. If you contact us by email, we receive whatever you choose to send and use it only to respond to you.

Third-party services we link to

The Site links to external services, including Eventbrite (event registration), WeFunder (investment campaign), StartupScience.io (founder platform), and social platforms such as LinkedIn, YouTube, X, and Spotify. If you follow those links, any information you provide is collected by those services under their own privacy policies, not this one. We encourage you to read them.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • We do not run advertising networks on the Site.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your choices

Because the Site does not maintain accounts or profiles about visitors, there is generally nothing for us to delete or correct. If you have emailed us and would like that correspondence deleted, ask and we will do so.

Changes

If the Site later adds features that change how information is handled (for example a newsletter signup or a shop checkout), this policy will be updated first, with a new "last updated" date above.
