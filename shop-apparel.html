About the Drop

Built like a company would.

Founder gear in 2025 is mostly hat-merch from defunct startups or Y Combinator hoodies from 2014. Nothing in between.

The first drop is three garments: hoodies, trucker hats, and t-shirts, each engineered for the actual wear-pattern of a working founder. Heavyweight cotton because demo days end at coffee shops. Premium fleece because pitch meetings happen in air conditioning. Structured caps because the camera's always on. One library of sayings, printed across all three. Production runs are small on purpose. Restocks are slow on purpose. The signal is the point.

  • Drop date: TBA, Q3 2025
  • Garments: 3 · 100+ designs
  • Tee fabric: 240gsm cotton · Hoodie fabric: 500gsm fleece
  • Sizes: XS–3XL · Ships from San Diego, CA · Returns: 30 days, no questions
