The Framework

Seven phases. One blueprint.

The Startup Lifecycle walks every founder through the same seven phases — the operating system behind twelve exits and the structure used inside accelerators, universities, and operator programs worldwide.

Vision & Northstar

Define what you're building and why it matters, and validate with advisors before scaling.

Product

Translate vision into a working MVP and refine it from early-adopter feedback.

Go-to-Market

Prove that acquisition strategies generate revenue at scale, establishing product–market fit.

Standardization

Document playbooks and workflows, reducing reliance on individuals.

Optimization

Refine processes and cut waste while tracking the KPIs that matter before capital injection.

Growth

Accelerate acquisition, partnerships, and geographic expansion while retaining operational excellence.

Exit

Execute the exit strategy years before signing, fostering acquirer relationships early.

Read the Book

Every phase, broken down with battle-tested strategies, founder interviews, and the artifacts to build at each stage.
