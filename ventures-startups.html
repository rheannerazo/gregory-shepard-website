The Exits

Twelve companies built. Twelve exits.

From 1994 through 2020: every company founded, scaled, and acquired.

Pepperjam

Performance marketing company offering brands an all-in-one approach to the modern affiliate channel. Became one of the three largest affiliate networks in the world.

Acquired by Partnerize · 2020

AdAssured

A SaaS compliance platform for search, domain, content, and coupon compliance, sold to networks and agencies to upgrade affiliate traffic quality.

Acquired by Pepperjam · 2016

AffiliateTraction

The first affiliate marketing agency, first to go international. Acquired by eBay Marketing Solutions as part of a $925M cross-border transaction, winner of 4 PE Awards.

Acquired by eBay Marketing Solutions · 2015

Affiliate Announcement

The web's most comprehensive full-service affiliate directory submission company, sold to a UK publicly traded business.

Acquired by IBG · 2006

EMagazines

A retail direct-to-consumer website offering virtually every magazine available: a new concept in an emerging digital subscription market.

Acquired · 2004

Alaya Labs

Manufacturer and online retailer of SAMe, a naturally occurring compound used in the treatment of osteoarthritis, liver disease, and depression.

Acquired · 2003

Magic Shine

Solved the problem of low quality, short battery life, and poor brightness in bicycle headlamps, growing to over 250,000 unit sales monthly.

Acquired by a China manufacturer · 2001

AllTravelInsurance.com

The first online travel insurance comparison website with the ability to purchase policies in real-time.

Acquired by an insurance carrier · 2001

AllPassportsAndVisas.com

A spinout from the travel insurance business, offering the fastest passport and visa turnaround without requiring customers to leave home.

Acquired by a service partner · 2001

AllCurrencyExchange

A second spinout from the travel insurance business: an international partnership providing local currency at better rates than airports or banks.

Acquired by Advanced Data Systems · 2001

Vision Environmental Solutions

Built around a highly concentrated microbial ecosystem engineered for maximum biodegradation efficiency of organic contaminants in wastewater.

Acquired by Epicore BioNetworks · 1997

American Federal Lending

A direct lender to the real estate industry, to agents and to borrowers backed by real estate. I was bought out in 1994.

Co-founder buyout · 1994
