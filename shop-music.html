About the Music

The soundtrack to the grind.

Most founder content is decks and podcasts. This is the other channel. The catalog is founder-centric from the first bar — songs about starting with nothing, building through the doubt, failing in public, and getting back up anyway. The sound moves across genres — reggae's patience and roots, hip hop's hustle and bars, and more — but the subject never changes: the lifecycle, set to music. Made for the headphones-on hours when the real work actually happens. Streaming everywhere soon. Built for the people doing the building.
