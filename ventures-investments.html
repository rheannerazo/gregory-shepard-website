The Investment Thesis

Operator capital, deployed with the framework.

Capital and operational expertise, on the cap table together.

My investments aren't passive checks. Founders who apply the seven-phase Startup Lifecycle have an empirically measurable advantage: three principles filter every deal.

  • Post product–market fit: the framework adds the most leverage once the product is already proving itself in market
  • Operator capital, not passive: every investment includes board work, framework deployment, and exit positioning
  • Altruistic capitalism: capital is a tool, not the goal; government modernization, clean infrastructure, and equitable access get preferential consideration
