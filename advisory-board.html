How It Works

Four conversations before a seat.

Fit matters more than speed.

Board fit is too consequential for a rushed process. Every potential engagement runs through the same assessment before I accept a formal role.

  • Discovery call: a 45-minute introduction with the founder, CEO, or lead investor
  • Due diligence: cap table, recent board decks, financials, and any active M&A activity
  • Founder fit: a working session on a real problem, testing chemistry both directions
  • Formal seat: engagement letter, equity terms, and onboarding, typically 30–45 days from first call
